Save the Farm

In order to live at peace again I have to at least try to live at my edge and save the land that I grew up on from getting clear cut and sold. The reason I'm striving to succeed in this endeavour is because this land has been a sanctuary for my family and for many others and it has been under our care since my great grandparents lived here and tending it since the early 1900's.

Initially I wanted to raise awareness and find ways to protect the habitat and people who live in this sacred place and I'm really hoping that by creating a cryptocurrency community I can use the technology to help protect other farm lands and families that are struggling as well. So far I have started a humanitarian effort to raise money to donate sustainable gift cards to families living in homeless shelters in my communtiy and have made efforts to clear out large amounts of trash that often goes unoticed off of the beach and out of national forest lands in my area. But it is hard to do this all on my own. Our future is counting on us, it is our right to step out of the dullness of inertia and to find ways to protect the sovereign rights of nature on our planet before they are completely stripped away.
